My Goals

Through my continuing education through Lambda School, I hope to land a job in the software development field, to continually improve on my knowledge and expertise and grow not just intellectually but all around.

Why software development?

I have always had an interest in computers. After a few months of self-teaching, I decided to enroll in Lambda School to push myself towards a career path I have long desired. Becoming a full-stack developer will allow me to work in a field that will consistently challenge my intellect, and allow me to continually grow and expand my knowledge in an constantly expanding field.
